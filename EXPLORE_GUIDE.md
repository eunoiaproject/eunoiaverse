### 4. **Where to Eat**

#### **Warung Makan Tradisional** (Budget)
- **Price**: $2-4 per meal
- **Cuisine**: Traditional local
- **Must Try**: 
  - Nasi Kuning (turmeric rice) - IDR 25k
  - Ikan Asap (smoked fish) - IDR 30k
  - Lumpia - IDR 3k
- **Rating**: 4.4/5 stars

#### **Mahakam Seafood Restaurant** (Mid-Range)
- **Price**: $5-10 per meal
- **Specialty**: Fresh river seafood
- **Must Try**:
  - Grilled Mahakam Fish - IDR 60k
  - Udang Bakar (grilled prawns) - IDR 50k
  - Soto Kuning - IDR 30k
- **Rating**: 4.7/5 stars

#### **Rumah Makan Dayak** (Authentic)
- **Price**: $4-7 per meal
- **Cuisine**: Traditional Dayak
- **Must Try**:
  - Lempah Kuning - IDR 45k
  - Ulat Sagu Bakar (grilled sago grubs) - IDR 30k
  - Tinutuan (rice porridge) - IDR 15k
- **Rating**: 4.8/5 stars

#### **Kutai Barat Cafe** (Modern)
- **Price**: $8-12 per meal
- **Cuisine**: Indonesian fusion with international options
- **Features**: Great coffee, WiFi available
- **Rating**: 4.3/5 stars

#### **Traditional Dishes Reference**

| Dish | Description | Price |
|------|-------------|-------|
| **Nasi Kuning** | Turmeric-flavored rice, staple dish | IDR 20-30k |
| **Ikan Asap** | Smoked fish from Mahakam River | IDR 25-40k |
| **Lempah Kuning** | Yellow turmeric soup with meat/seafood | IDR 30-45k |
| **Ulat Sagu** | Sago grubs - protein-rich delicacy | IDR 25-30k |
| **Tinutuan** | Rice porridge comfort food | IDR 10-15k |
| **Perkedel Goreng** | Fried potato croquettes | IDR 5k |
| **Kare Ayam** | Chicken curry | IDR 20-25k |

#### **Dining Tips**
- Eat at warungs for authentic food and low prices
- Bring hand sanitizer for hygiene
- Stick to bottled or boiled water
- Most places accept cash only (IDR)
- Meal times: Breakfast 6-9am, Lunch 11:30am-2pm, Dinner 6-9pm
