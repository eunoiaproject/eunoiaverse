Traditional Dishes to Try

Nasi Kuning

Turmeric-flavored rice, staple dish of the region

Ikan Asap

Smoked fish from Mahakam River, delicious when grilled

Lempah Kuning

Yellow turmeric-based soup with meat or seafood

Ulat Sagu

Sago grubs - a protein-rich jungle delicacy

Tinutuan

Rice porridge, comfort food for any time

Dining Tips

  • Eat at warungs (local eateries) for authentic food and low prices
  • Bring hand sanitizer - not all places have extensive hygiene standards
  • Water: Stick to bottled or boiled water
  • Most restaurants don't accept cards - bring cash (IDR)
  • Meal times: Breakfast 6-9am, Lunch 11:30am-2pm, Dinner 6-9pm
